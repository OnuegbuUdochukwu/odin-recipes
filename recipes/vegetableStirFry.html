How to Make Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish
  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, honey, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add minced garlic and ginger, and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add sliced bell pepper, broccoli florets, julienned carrot, snap peas, and sliced mushrooms to the skillet. Stir-fry for 3-5 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Pour the prepared sauce over the vegetables in the skillet. Cook, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens and coats the vegetables evenly.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat and transfer the Vegetable Stir Fry to a serving dish.
  6. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve hot over steamed rice or noodles.
