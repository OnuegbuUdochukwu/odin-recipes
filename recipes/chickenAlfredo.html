How to make Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fettuccine noodles
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, diced
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  1. Cook the fettuccine noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Slowly pour in the heavy cream, stirring constantly. Allow the mixture to simmer until it starts to thicken, about 3-5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add the cooked chicken breast to the sauce and stir until heated through.
  6. Transfer the cooked fettuccine noodles to the skillet with the sauce. Toss until the noodles are evenly coated.
  7. Serve the Chicken Alfredo hot, garnished with freshly chopped parsley.
